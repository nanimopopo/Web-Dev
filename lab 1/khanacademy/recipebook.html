Classic Guacamole

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Ripe Avocados 3
Lime Juice 1 tbsp
Red Onion (minced) 1/4 cup

Instructions

Step 1: Mash the avocados in a bowl until they reach your desired consistency (chunky or smooth).

Step 2: Stir in the lime juice, salt, onions, and cilantro.

Step 3: Serve immediately with tortilla chips.

Simple Tomato Soup

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Canned Tomatoes 28 oz
Vegetable Broth 1 cup
Heavy Cream 1/4 cup

Instructions

  1. Simmer the tomatoes and broth in a pot for 20 minutes.
  2. Blend the mixture until smooth using an immersion blender.
  3. Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt and pepper.

Source: Food Network
